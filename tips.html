Tips

Vrindavan:

1. Participate in the vibrant and spiritual atmosphere by joining local rituals and ceremonies.

2. Explore the narrow lanes and markets to discover local crafts and traditional items.

3. Respect the sanctity of the religious sites and follow any dress codes in temples.

Haridwar:

1. Attend the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri for a spiritual experience.

2. Take a cable car ride to Mansa Devi Temple for panoramic views of the city.

3. Explore the nearby Rajaji National Park for wildlife enthusiasts.
